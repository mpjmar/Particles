LEVELS QUICK GUIDE

Click energy nodes to recharge your ability. Click the board to trigger your ability.

Charges: total stored charges. Spendable: charges available right now.

Reserve Lock: minimum charges kept in reserve. If charges are at or below this value, ability use is blocked.

Tip: adjust Reserve Lock with [-] and [+] in the HUD, or keyboard [ and ] during Levels mode.
