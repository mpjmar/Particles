PARTICLES QUICK GUIDE

ENGLISH

  • Simulation: watch-only mode. No manual clicks required.
  • Pulse Clash: configurable PvE mode (board, speed, density, spawn tuning).
  • Core Ascension: level-based campaign with escalating objectives.
  • Core click abilities: Photon click = explosion (kills nearby Electrons), Electron click = spawn Electron clusters (to hunt Photons).
  • Charges: both sides have max 5 charges. Recover by clicking Energy Nodes.
  • Reserve Lock: protects emergency charges. At/below lock, ability is blocked.
  • Quick controls: adjust Reserve Lock with HUD [-]/[+] or Left/Right arrows.
  • Tip: start with Reserve Lock at 1-2 for safer ability usage.

ESPANOL

  • Simulation: modo de observacion. No requiere clic manual.
  • Pulse Clash: modo PvE configurable (tablero, velocidad, densidad y aparicion).
  • Core Ascension: campana por niveles con objetivos cada vez mas exigentes.
  • Habilidad por clic en Core: Photon = explosion en area (elimina Electrones cercanos), Electron = crea clusters de Electrones (para cazar Photons).
  • Cargas: ambos bandos tienen maximo 5. Se recargan clicando Nodos de Energia.
  • Reserve Lock: guarda cargas de emergencia. En ese minimo o por debajo, la habilidad se bloquea.
  • Controles rapidos: ajusta Reserve Lock con HUD [-]/[+] o flechas Izquierda/Derecha.
  • Consejo: empieza con Reserve Lock en 1-2 para usar habilidades con mas seguridad.

Need a quick rule? Prioritize Energy Nodes, then spend charges where pressure is highest.
